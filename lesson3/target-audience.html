Target Audience

The target audience for Mountain Valley Chamber is the small business of the comnmunities comprising of Herriman, Riverton and Bluffdale. A small business would be any company with less than 100 employees. The target audience includes anyone with a registered business, regardless of age, gender or sexual orientation, that is seeking to connect with the other businesses in the community and to expand their own.

Two Personas

Maise is a single mother of five kids, ranging in age from 20 to 11. She immigrated from Venezuela 19 years earlier and now has her own house cleaning business called EcoClean, it has been opened and providing services for homes in Utah valley for 10 years. She would like to find out how she can better promote her local business, her employment openings, and connect with other Hispanic business owners in the area.

Kevin is a young entrepreneur who just graduated from college as a business major. They just opened their own taco truck and would like to join the chamber of commerce to help get their business name out there and join in on community events that will help them grow their customer base to more than just their family. They will be using the website to join the commerce, find contact information for event organizers that may be looking for food trucks and for easy access to the paper work that is needed to participate in the events.
