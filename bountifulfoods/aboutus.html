About Us

Our Mission

We deliver fresh fruit and produce directly from the hands of our local farmers and sometimes international suppliers, to your kitchen table.

In 2014, Rita Harley was frustrated at the fact that to eat healthy you had to making more money or risk not being able to pay your bills so she set out to find a solution. In 2015 she decided to be the solution and founded Bountiful Foods! Hoping to be able to reach as many people as possible, Bountiful Foods opened a location in the heart of Carlsbad, California to welcome in locals and tourists but also caters through food trucks to the entirity of south California. So if you're looking for fresh organic fruit, produce and smoothies come visit us today!
