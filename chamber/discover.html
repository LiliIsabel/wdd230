Discover Herriman, Riverton and Bluffdale!
With a combined population of over 103,806 we have a thriving community with lots to do and see! Herriman (population 45,211), Riverton (43,793) and Bluffdale (14,793) have seen incredible growth over the last few years as we work to become the ideal place to be!
The economy in our community is booming with the median household income being $102,096 for Herriman, $101,810 for Riverton and $113,023 for Bluffdale! The largest industries within our communities are Retail trade, Profesional, Scientific and Technical Services and Health Care and Social Assistance.
Our beautiful cities can be found in the southwestern corner of the Salt Lake Valley.
If you enjoy nature then you will be happy to hear we are home many trails for biking and walking with beautiful views.
Stay close to home and find everything you need! We have over a hundred businesses including 365 Golf, 9Rounds, Counseling Services, ABC Great Beginings daycare, Walmart, Home Depot, Costco, a brand new Cinemark and more coming soon!
If you have little ones attending school within one of our great cities, they will be a part of the Jordan School district! The Jordan School districts average test ranking is 6/10 placing us among the top 50% of public schools in Utah. The Public schools within our district have an average math proficiency score of 45% and reading proficiency score of 47% with a graduation rate of 87%.
